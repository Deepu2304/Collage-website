VISION

"To be a leading center of innovation and excellence in the field of robotics and automation, pioneering advancements that benefit stakeholders of MLRITM, society, industry, and technology."

2016
RAL(Robotics and Automation Lab)

Established in 2016, RAL pioneers robotics and automation technology. Our culture fosters innovation, collaboration, and diversity. We aim to revolutionize industries, enhance human capabilities, and drive progress. Join us in shaping the future of robotics and automation. Explore endless possibilities with RAL.

2024
Indefatigable journey of 8 years

At RAL, we're pioneers in robotics and automation. With state and national victories, including IIT Hyderabad, CBIT, and Sreenidhi fests, we redefine possibilities. Our commitment to excellence drives innovation, transforming industries. Join us to explore technology's power and the thrill of success. Welcome to RAL, where greatness knows no limits.
